<p>On the 25th of April, SEARADO had its Board meeting with a full attendance
together with the Operations Points of Contacts of some NADOs, World Anti-Doping
Agency (WADA) representatives and the Japan Anti-Doping Agency (JADA),
SEARADO's MoU partner.</p>
<p>Apart from the updates from WADA and SEARADO, there was sharing by JADA
on a decade of collaboration and support; sharing by Anti-Doping Malaysia
(ADAMAS) on the following: Social Science Research Symposium done in 2024;
formation of an Athlete Commission in ADAMAS and an upcoming symposium
on Anti-Doping and Sports Integrity. There were also sharing sessions by
the Lao National Anti-Doping Organisation (LAONADO) and the Vietnam Anti-Doping
Agency (VADA) on their achievements through a MoU with the Doping Control
Agency of Thailand (DCAT) and the China National Anti-Doping Agency (CHINADA)
respectively.</p>

<p>SEARADO also had the opportunity to hear from the guest speaker, Prof
Nathinee Panvisavas, Director of the National Doping Control Centre (NDCC),
Mahidol University, who provided an overview of services provided by the
laboratory and great tips to ensure processes such as shipping goes smoothly.
SEARADO would also like to thank Dr Hilary Inwood, Deputy Governor of Sports,
Thailand for gracing the occasion and Mr Nuttavuth Ruangvech, National
Olympic Committee of Thailand representative for his presence and support
for Clean Sport.</p>
