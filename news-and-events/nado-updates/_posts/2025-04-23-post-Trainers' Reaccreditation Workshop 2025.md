<p>The start of a busy schedule of Clean Sport activities for the week organised
by SEARADO started on 22nd April 2025. The SEARADO Trainer Re-accreditation
Workshop was held in Bangkok, Thailand with the support of the Doping Control
Agency of Thailand.</p>
<p>Since 2014, these SEARADO SCP Trainers have been trained to conduct Doping
Control Officer (DCO) courses, DCO re-accreditation courses, Blood Collection
Officers (BCO) courses and BCO re-accreditation courses. The team of dedicated
SEARADO Trainers have been conducting the courses for member countries
over the years. In order to keep them abreast of the latest information
on testing and enhancing their capacity in presentation and other soft
skills, SEARADO continues a re-accreditation process once in 2 years. The
trainers were given an insight into the upcoming changes to the International
Standards of Testing (IST), reaccreditation approaches from the Japan Anti-Doping
Agency (JADA) and China Anti-Doping Agency (CHINADA), raising their bar
in presentations.</p>
<p>SEARADO thanks Ms Chika Hirai from JADA, who has been the Advisor to the
Trainers team and her support for the workshop, as well as, Mr Zhou Weicheng,
from CHINADA for his sharing at the workshop.</p>
