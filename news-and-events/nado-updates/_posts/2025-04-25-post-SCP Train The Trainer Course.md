<p>With almost all SEARADO member countries having an established testing
team within their NADOs, SEARADO organised a Sample Collection Personnel
(SCP) Train-The-Trainer Course with the support of DCAT Doping Control
Agency of Thailand on the 23rd and 24th of April in Bangkok, Thailand.</p>
<p>SEARADO congratulates the 9 participants from the member countries for
their active participation throughout the course and wish them all the
best as SCP Trainers.</p>
