<p></p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/JADA Seminar/410345990_724943923002320_3536115828492627501_n.jpg"></div><p></p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/JADA Seminar/WhatsApp_Image_2023_12_20_at_10_52_43.jpeg"></div><p>SEARADO would like to thank the Japan Anti-Doping Agency (JADA) for organising another successful 2023 International Seminar with the main objectives of strengthening the capacity of the Clean Sport Community in Asia &amp; Oceania; implementing a better practice going beyond the minimum requirements; and build the Clean Sport Community through networking and strengthening partnerships.</p><p>The seminar achieved its objectives and also provided participants to discuss and put forth possible changes to the upcoming revision of the Code, as well as, an opportunity to put forth queries to the management team of WADA.</p><p></p><p>A customised workshop was planned and put together with Japan Anti-Doping Agency (JADA), Central Asia RADO (RADOCA) and SEARADO as a follow up of to the Anti-Doping Education Symposium in Singapore. The workshop provided a platform for all participants to share their education plan and new initiatives taken. Participants were also given an insight on some detail aspects of education and best practices.</p>
