<p>The World Anti-Doping Agency (WADA) today has published the 2025 List
of Prohibited Substances and Methods, <u>which will come into effect on the 1st of January 2025</u>.
<br>
<br>In addition, the&nbsp;<a href="https://www.wada-ama.org/sites/default/files/2024-09/2025_list_explanatory_note_en_final_11_september_2024.pdf" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">2025 Summary of Major Modifications</a>,
as well as, the&nbsp;<a href="https://www.wada-ama.org/sites/default/files/2024-09/2025_list_monitoring_program_en_final_clean_11_september_2024.pdf" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">2025 Monitoring Program</a>,
have also been published as well.
<br>
<br>NADOs are strongly encouraged to read through the major changes and also
inform relevant stakeholders.
<br>
<br>More information can be found on <a href="https://www.instagram.com/wada_ama/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.wada-ama.org/en/resources/2025-prohibited-list</a>
</p>
